The **Red Herb** is a healing booster in Resident Evil Requiem. It has no effect on its own, but when combined with a Green Herb it creates a powerful healing mixture.

## Location

Red Herbs are found throughout the facility but are less common than Green Herbs. Search optional areas and hidden rooms.

## Effect

Boosts healing when combined with a Green Herb.

## Usage

Combine a Red Herb with a Green Herb to fully restore health and temporarily increase your maximum health. This is the most efficient use of herbs in the game.
