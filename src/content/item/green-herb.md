The **Green Herb** is the standard healing consumable in Resident Evil Requiem. It restores a small amount of health and can be combined with Red Herbs for enhanced healing.

## Location

Green Herbs are found throughout the facility, most commonly in planters, lockers, and drawers near safe rooms.

## Effect

Restores a small amount of health.

## Usage

Use a Green Herb from the inventory to restore health, or combine it with a Red Herb for a stronger healing effect that can also temporarily increase your maximum health.
