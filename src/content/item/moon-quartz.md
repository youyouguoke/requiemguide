The **Moon Quartz** is a key item in Resident Evil Requiem. It is one of several quartz keys needed to solve the **Moon Quartz Box puzzle** and open the path through the Central Hall.

## Location

Moon Quartz is found in **Chapter 3, Quartz Path**. Look for it on a pedestal after clearing the enemy encounter in the area.

## Effect

Used in the **Moon Quartz Box puzzle**.

## Usage

Insert the Moon Quartz into the Moon Quartz Box along with the other quartz keys to unlock the gate. This is required to progress through the Central Hall.
