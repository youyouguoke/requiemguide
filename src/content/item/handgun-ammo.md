**Handgun Ammo** is the standard 9mm resource in Resident Evil Requiem. It is used by the B934 and S&S M232 handguns.

## Location

Handgun Ammo is a common resource found in lockers, drawers, and as drops from defeated enemies.

## Effect

Ammo for handguns.

## Usage

Load Handgun Ammo into the B934 or S&S M232. It is the most common ammo type, making it ideal for standard enemies and light puzzles.
