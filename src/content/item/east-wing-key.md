The **East Wing Key** is a key item in Resident Evil Requiem. It unlocks the main corridor door and is required to progress from the East Wing into the rest of the facility.

## Location

Found on the **reception desk in the East Wing lobby** at the start of **Chapter 1**.

## Effect

Unlocks the **main corridor** door.

## Usage

Use the East Wing Key on the locked door in the East Wing lobby to exit the area and continue into the West Wing.
