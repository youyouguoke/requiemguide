The **Central Hall Quartz Mechanism** combines the three Quartz pieces you've collected. It is located in the Central Hall, the large chamber connecting the East and West Wings of the Care Center.

## Common Mistakes

- Trying to insert the Quartz pieces in the wrong order: the order does not matter, but each piece must go in its matching slot.
- Forgetting to bring Emily for the Star Quartz: if you left her behind, backtrack to find her.
