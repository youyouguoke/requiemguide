The **Star Quartz Puzzle Box** is the last Quartz puzzle needed for the Central Hall mechanism. It is located in the Security Manager's Office on the first floor of the East Wing.

## Where to Find the Level 2 Wristband

The Level 2 Wristband is located in the Security Office locker. You need the basic East Wing access to reach it, which is obtained naturally through story progression.

## Reward

You receive the **Star Quartz**, the final piece for the Central Hall door. Once you have all three Quartz pieces, return to the Central Hall mechanism.

## Tips

- Make sure Emily is with you before entering the Security Manager's Office.
- If Emily is lost or separated, backtrack to find her; the puzzle cannot be completed without her.
- This is a good time to stock up on supplies before heading back to the Central Hall.
