The **Moon Quartz Puzzle Box** is the first Quartz puzzle needed to unlock the Central Hall door. It is found in the Chairman's Office on the second floor of the West Wing.

## Where to Find the Pencil

The Pencil is on the same desk as the torn notebook. It is impossible to miss if you thoroughly search the office. Make sure to pick up any nearby files that add lore about the Care Center's history.

## Reward

You receive the **Moon Quartz**, one of three required pieces for the Central Hall mechanism. Without this item, you cannot proceed to the courtyard.

## Tips

- Search the office thoroughly before leaving; there is also a file with lore about Elena.
- If you forget the sequence, check your inventory notes or return to the notebook.
- The box itself is not guarded, but the West Wing has more enemies than the East Wing.
