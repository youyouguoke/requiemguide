The **Blood Analyzer** puzzles are logic-grid challenges found in the Blood Lab. Solving them unlocks crafting recipes and useful supplies that help you survive the later chapters.

## Why These Puzzles Matter

The crafting recipes unlocked here allow you to craft powerful healing items and ammo later in the game. Missing them makes the final chapters noticeably harder, so they are recommended for most players.
