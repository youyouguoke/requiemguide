The **Titan Spinner** is a massive, arachnid-like bioweapon that serves as one of the late-game boss encounters for Leon. It combines speed, poison, and extremely high health, making it one of the toughest fights in the game.

## Overview

The Titan Spinner is fought in the underground sewers near the Elpis containment chamber. Its body is heavily armored except for its multiple eyes and the soft underside of its abdomen. It has several phases and becomes increasingly aggressive as its health drops.

## Reward

Defeating the Titan Spinner drops the **Facility Master Key**, granting access to the Elpis containment chamber and the final story choices.
