The **Blister Borne** is a grotesque, spiny mutant that Leon fights shortly after meeting Grace and Emily at the church. Its body is covered in pulsing orange cysts that serve as its primary weak points.

## Overview

The Blister Borne is a mid-sized boss with high mobility and a disturbing design. It relies on quick lunges and projectile attacks, making it important to keep your distance while targeting its cysts. The fight takes place in the church courtyard, which provides enough space to circle the boss but little cover.

## Reward

Defeating the Blister Borne unlocks the church exit and allows Leon, Grace, and Emily to continue toward the Care Center.
