The **Shadow Ghost**, also known as **The Girl**, is the first major boss Grace faces in *Resident Evil Requiem*. It stalks the dark corridors of the Rhodes Hill Care Center and reacts aggressively to sound.

## Overview

The Shadow Ghost is a tall, pale figure with long white hair and a distorted face. Unlike most bosses, it cannot be killed through raw damage alone in the early encounters; instead, Grace must use light to repel it. The final battle takes place in the Water Treatment Plant, where overhead lamps and flares become crucial tools.

## Reward

Defeating or repelling the Shadow Ghost in the Water Treatment Plant allows Grace to exit the facility and progress to the next chapter.
