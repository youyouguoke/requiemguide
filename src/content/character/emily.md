Emily is a key survivor Grace encounters inside the Rhodes Hill Care Center. She is visually impaired and relies on Grace to guide her through the facility. Her fate is one of the central emotional threads of the story.

## Background

Emily is a young girl who survived the initial outbreak. She is unable to see, but she has an uncanny ability to read Braille symbols that are important to several puzzles. Grace takes responsibility for protecting her, and their bond becomes one of the driving forces of the narrative.

## Key Traits

- **Role**: Survivor, puzzle helper, emotional core
- **Ability**: Reads Braille symbols needed for the Star Quartz puzzle
- **First Appearance**: Chapter 2 at the church
- **Vulnerability**: Cannot defend herself; must be protected by Grace

## Story Role

Emily's fate is tied to the game's branching endings. Depending on player choices, she may be saved, or mutate into a powerful boss that Leon must fight. Protecting her is key to unlocking the "Save Emily" ending path.

## Notable Details

- Emily can read Braille symbols in the Security Manager's Office, which is needed for the Star Quartz puzzle.
- Her transformation represents one of the tragic turning points of the story.
- If she is harmed in the research wing, she transforms into the boss Emily (Transformed).
- She is referenced in the final Elpis choice regardless of whether she survives.
