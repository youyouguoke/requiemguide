Grace Ashcroft is the primary protagonist of **Resident Evil Requiem**. She awakens inside the Rhodes Hill Care Center with no memory of how she arrived, and must navigate the facility's horrors while uncovering her connection to the outbreak.

## Background

Grace is resourceful and stealth-focused, often avoiding direct confrontation. She relies on light-based mechanics and environmental puzzles to survive, in contrast to Leon's more action-oriented segments. Her journey is as much about discovering her own identity as it is about escaping the facility.

## Key Traits

- **Playstyle**: Stealth, light manipulation, puzzle-solving
- **Signature Enemy**: The Shadow Ghost (The Girl)
- **Weapon Affinity**: Handguns, especially the S&S M232
- **Strengths**: Quiet movement, environmental awareness, puzzle intuition
- **Weaknesses**: Limited direct combat ability, reliance on light sources

## Story Role

Grace serves as the player's introduction to the world of Resident Evil Requiem. Through her eyes, we experience the first outbreaks, meet Emily, and learn about the mysterious Elena / Elpis. Her choices about whether to protect Emily and how to handle Elpis directly influence the game's ending.

## Notable Equipment

- **B934**: Starting handgun
- **S&S M232**: Preferred handgun for most of the campaign
- **Requiem**: High-damage rifle obtained in the mid-game
- **Flashlight**: Essential tool for the Shadow Ghost encounters
