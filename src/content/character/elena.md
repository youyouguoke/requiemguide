Elena, also known as **Elpis**, is the mysterious catalyst behind the events at Rhodes Hill. Uncovering her identity and deciding her fate is central to the game's final act and endings.

## Background

Elena was once a patient or test subject at the Rhodes Hill Care Center. Experiments performed on her led to the creation of Elpis, a bioweapon or mutated entity that is connected to the outbreak. By the time the player reaches the Elpis laboratory, Elena is barely human and her consciousness is merged with the entity.

## Key Traits

- **Role**: Antagonist, tragic figure, final choice catalyst
- **First Appearance**: Late game in the Elpis Laboratory
- **Connection**: Linked to Grace's past and the origin of the outbreak
- **Entity**: Elpis is the bioweapon manifestation of Elena

## Story Role

The final choice of the game revolves around Elena / Elpis. The player must decide whether to release her, believing she can be saved, or destroy her to end the threat. This decision, combined with whether Emily survived, determines which ending the player sees.

## Key Choices

- **Release Elena**: Triggers one ending path where Elpis escapes into the world.
- **Destroy Elpis**: Leads to an alternate outcome where the facility collapses but the threat is ended.

These decisions, combined with whether Emily survives, shape the game's conclusion.

## Notable Details

- Audio logs and research files in the underground lab reveal Elena's history.
- Her relationship to Grace is hinted at throughout the game through files and cutscenes.
- The name "Elpis" is likely a reference to the Greek spirit of hope, adding a tragic layer to her story.
