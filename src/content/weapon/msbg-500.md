The **MSBG 500** is a pump-action shotgun used by Leon. It is found near the kitchen where Leon first encounters Chunk, and it remains a staple for Leon's combat-heavy sections throughout the game.

## Best Use Cases

- Close-range crowd control.
- Staggering bosses like Chunk and Blister Borne.
- Dealing with groups of fast enemies.
- Finishing off enemies that get too close.

## Tips

Aim for the head to maximize damage and stagger. Reloading between shots requires timing, so don't get surrounded. Keep a handgun ready for distant enemies, as the shotgun's effective range is limited.
