The **S&S M232** is widely considered the best handgun for Grace in Resident Evil Requiem. It outperforms the starting B934 in every category and allows consistent headshot staggers.

## Best Use Cases

- Standard enemy crowd control with headshots.
- Staggering enemies to set up melee follow-ups.
- The Shadow Ghost fight when it is stunned by light.
- Ammo-efficient clearing of rooms.

## Tips

- Prioritize stability upgrades before power.
- Use it as your main weapon for most of Grace's campaign.
- Save 9mm ammo for the M232 rather than wasting it on the B934 after you switch.
- After installing stabilizers, a single weak-point shot will stagger most enemies.
