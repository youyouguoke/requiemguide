The **Kotetsu** is a post-game melee weapon that never breaks. It is an excellent choice for replaying the game on higher difficulties or tackling challenge modes without worrying about durability.

## Best Use Cases

- Ammo conservation on standard difficulty replays.
- Challenge modes where resources are limited.
- Close-quarters ambushes when you want to save gun ammo.

## Tips

- Use it to conserve ammo on standard difficulty replays.
- Pair with movement upgrades to close gaps quickly.
- Be careful against ranged or flying enemies; the Kotetsu has no projectile capability.
- It is not ideal for boss fights unless you are very confident in your melee timing.
