The **B934** is Grace's default handgun in Resident Evil Requiem. It is serviceable in the early chapters but should be replaced by the **S&S M232** once you reach the West Wing.

## Best Use Cases

- Clearing common zombies in the early chapters.
- Shooting environmental objects and light puzzles.
- Conserving shotgun and rifle ammo for tougher enemies.

## Tips

- Use it for common zombies and light puzzles.
- Don't invest too many resources in upgrades; save them for the M232.
- Switch to the M232 as soon as possible for better stagger potential.
- Headshots still matter with the B934, but its low stability makes rapid fire less accurate.
