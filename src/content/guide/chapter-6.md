Chapter 6 is the final chapter of Resident Evil Requiem. It features the last boss fights and the branching endings based on your choices throughout the game.

## Objectives

- Fight the **Titan Spinner** in the underground sewers.
- Reach the Elpis containment chamber.
- Make the final choice: **Release or Destroy Elpis**.
- Survive the closing sequence to view the ending.

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### 1. Sewer Approach

The chapter begins in the underground sewers. The path is linear but filled with hazards, including poison gas vents and ambushes. Move slowly, shoot gas valves to clear toxic clouds, and conserve powerful ammo for the boss.

### 2. Boss: Titan Spinner

The Titan Spinner is a massive, arachnid-like bioweapon that blocks the path to the Elpis chamber. Target its eyes and abdomen for critical damage. The Requiem rifle and explosive rounds are highly effective.

Its attack phases include:

- **Web spray**: Slows movement; dodge sideways.
- **Pounce**: Roll or sprint laterally to avoid.
- **Poison AoE**: Back away when it rears up; the poison cloud lingers.

After defeating it, collect the **Facility Master Key** and proceed to the Elpis containment chamber.

### 3. Elpis Containment Chamber

In the chamber, you finally confront Elena / Elpis. The game presents two choices:

- **Release Elpis**: Believing Elena can be saved, you release her. This leads to an ending where Elpis escapes and may trigger a new bioterror threat.
- **Destroy Elpis**: You terminate the threat, causing the facility to collapse. This leads to a more decisive but tragic ending.

Your choice here, combined with whether Emily survived, determines which ending you see.

### 4. Closing Sequence

After the choice, a timed escape sequence begins. Follow the marker, avoid falling debris, and reach the extraction point. The final cutscene plays based on your ending flags.

## Key Tips

- Use explosive and high-penetration weapons on the Titan Spinner.
- Your choices regarding Emily and Elpis determine which ending you see.
- Save before the final choice if you want to see multiple endings.
- During the escape sequence, don't stop to fight; just run.
