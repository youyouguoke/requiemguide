Chapter 5 reveals the truth behind the outbreak as the group descends into the **underground research facility**. The narrative branches based on whether Emily survived the earlier chapters.

## Objectives

- Explore the Blood Lab and solve the **Blood Analyzer** puzzles.
- Obtain the **Level 3 Wristband**.
- Survive the underground facility's tougher enemies.
- Confront Emily's fate.
- Learn about **Elena / Elpis** and the experiments that created the outbreak.

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### 1. Underground Entrance

After the courtyard rendezvous, Leon and Grace descend into the facility through a hidden elevator. The underground area is heavily populated with advanced infected and a new enemy type that can crawl on walls.

### 2. Blood Lab and Analyzer Puzzles

The Blood Lab contains two optional Blood Analyzer puzzles:

- **Denatured Blood**: Click the central node. Reward: Handgun Ammo + Hemolytic Injector crafting recipe.
- **Reversible Blood**: Follow the path Bottom-left → Middle → Top-right → 2nd from top. Reward: Steroids + crafting recipe.

These puzzles are optional but provide valuable crafting unlocks and resources.

### 3. Level 3 Wristband

The **Level 3 Wristband** is required to open the secure research wing. Find it in the locker room near the security checkpoint. This wristband also opens several optional storage rooms containing high-tier ammo and healing items.

### 4. Emily's Fate

This is the major branching point of the chapter. If Emily was kept alive and protected, she remains with the group. If she was harmed or left behind in earlier chapters, she mutates and becomes a boss fight for Leon.

- **If Emily survives**: She helps open the final door to the Elpis laboratory.
- **If Emily transforms**: Leon must fight **Emily (Transformed)**. See the boss guide for strategy.

### 5. The Elpis Laboratory

The final area of the chapter reveals Elena, also known as Elpis. Listen to the audio logs and read the research files to understand the experiments. The chapter ends with a choice that sets up the final act.

## Key Tips

- The Blood Analyzer puzzles are optional but rewarding.
- If Emily is still alive, protect her in the research wing; losing her here locks the "Save Emily" ending.
- Stock up on healing and magnum ammo before the next chapter.
- Read the research files for lore on Elpis and the outbreak origin.
