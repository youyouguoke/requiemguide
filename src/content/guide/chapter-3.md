Chapter 3 focuses on Grace collecting the three **Quartz pieces** scattered across the Care Center to unlock the Central Hall door and reach the courtyard.

## Objectives

- Find the **Pencil** and solve the **Moon Quartz Box** in the Chairman's Office.
- Retrieve the corrosive and open the **Sun Quartz Box** in the Lead Researcher's Office.
- Obtain the **Level 2 Wristband** and bring Emily to the **Star Quartz Box**.
- Insert all three Quartz pieces into the **Central Hall mechanism**.
