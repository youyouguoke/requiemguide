The **Blister Borne Minion** is a mini-boss swarm enemy in Resident Evil Requiem. They spawn during the Blister Borne boss fight and can overwhelm the player if ignored.

## Behavior

These small creatures swarm during the Blister Borne fight. They are fast and can overwhelm the player if ignored.

## Weakness

Fire and explosive damage deal massive area damage to the swarm, killing multiple minions at once.
