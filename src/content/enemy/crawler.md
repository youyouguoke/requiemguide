The **Crawler** is a common enemy in Resident Evil Requiem. It moves slowly along floors and walls and is especially dangerous in dark areas.

## Behavior

Crawlers are slow but hard to spot in dark corners. Their scraping sound is a clear warning of their presence.

## Weakness

Headshots and fire deal extra damage. Their low profile makes them harder to hit than standing enemies.
