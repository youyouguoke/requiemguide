The **Shambler** is the most common enemy in Resident Evil Requiem. It is slow, noise-reactive, and easy to kill with a single headshot.

## Behavior

Shamblers are slow, noise-reactive infected. They are usually found in groups and will converge on any loud noise.

## Weakness

Headshots deal critical damage and are the most efficient way to kill them.
