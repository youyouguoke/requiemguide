The **Howler** is a common but high-threat enemy in Resident Evil Requiem. It screams to alert nearby enemies and can quickly turn a quiet room into a swarm.

## Behavior

Howlers scream to alert nearby enemies. They are fast and should be prioritized before engaging other targets.

## Weakness

Flashlight stun. A flashlight stun interrupts their scream and leaves them vulnerable for a headshot or melee follow-up.
